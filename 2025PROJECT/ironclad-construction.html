Built on Trust,
Delivered with Precision

Founded in 1996, D.N.A. Builders Construction Group has grown from a local contractor into one of the region's most trusted construction firms. We specialize in delivering complex projects on time and on budget — from high-rise commercial towers to sprawling industrial estates.

Our team of over 350 engineers, architects, project managers, and skilled tradespeople shares a single commitment: building structures that stand the test of time, and relationships that outlast any contract.

Zero-incident culture with rigorous OSHA-compliant protocols on every site.

The D.N.A. Builders Difference

We don't just build structures — we build confidence. Here's what separates us from every other contractor you'll consider.

Start Your Project
01

In-House Engineering Team

Our 80+ licensed engineers and architects are full-time employees — not subcontracted. That means faster decisions, consistent quality, and direct accountability on every project.

02

Fixed-Price Contracts

No surprise invoices. We provide comprehensive fixed-price contracts so you know exactly what you're getting and what it costs before we break ground.

03

BIM-Driven Execution

We use Building Information Modeling across all projects, allowing real-time clash detection, accurate costing, and a fully coordinated build process from day one.

04

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our quality management system is ISO-certified and independently audited annually, ensuring every process meets the highest international benchmarks.

05

Post-Handover Support

Our relationship doesn't end at turnover. We provide a 12-month defects liability period plus optional long-term maintenance contracts for all completed works.
